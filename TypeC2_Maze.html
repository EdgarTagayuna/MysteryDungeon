Thorns

Traversing the pathway, it seemed to strech endlessly towards a very dark, singular point.
As you continued on foot, all you could hear was your footsteps and the sound of your heart beating.
Just as you were walking, the ground suddenly crumbled and gave away, resulting in you falling down from a pretty high place.
In an instance, everything was becoming a blur. At that moment, you realized that huge thorns portruding from the ground
pierced through your body. You coughed up blood, with your vision becoming even blurrier. In the end, you lost consciousness,
and died.


You shouldn't have entered in the first place. May this be a lesson to future travelers.
