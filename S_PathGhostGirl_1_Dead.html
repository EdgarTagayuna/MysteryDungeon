Eternal Darkness

As you looked around, your surroundings became darker and darker. Everything got cold. You felt yourself losing conciousness.
With your body feeling a lot heavier than usual, you fell down to the ground. From the corners of your eyes, you noticed someone.
It was the girl from before. You saw her smirking with her eyes nowhere to be seen. With your eyes getting heavier and heavier, you fell
into an eternal slumber.


May you slumber in peace, young soul.
