Monster Encounter: Giant Rock Golem

As you walked towards the hallway, the walls and the floor further in front of you suddenly crumbled.
You saw a giant, rock-like hand coming out of the crumbling wall. Then its entirety showed up, causing the
surrounding area to give away. You fell, and so was the giant. Upon landing, you noticed a 20ft tall rock golem
towering in front of you. Its body structure was similar to that of a human's. The only difference was that
it had no face, and its entire body was made out of rock or stone of some kind. At the center of its body, there was a
crimson core glowing brightly. The golem proceeded to lower itself on all fours with its knees supporting it, giving it a total of
12ft height distribution. It seemed like it was about to charge in like a bull. You looked around, but there didn't seem to be a way out. As it charged
towards you, the giant golem was heading in a linear movement. It didn't seem like it could turn very quickly either.
What will you do, traveler?

Evade right
Evade left
Evade upwards, climbing on top of it.
