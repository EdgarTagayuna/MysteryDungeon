Caught by the attack

As you evaded to the side, the giant rock golem caught you with its arm, crashing against the wall.
It then proceeded to stand up, with you in its hands. You felt the grip tightened along your ribs, gradually
crushing you until your spine snapped. In an instant, you then exploded into a pool of blood as it crushed
your entirety in the golem's extremely tight grip.


Quite the unfortunate turn of events. May no traveler ever attempt to venture this dungeon again.
