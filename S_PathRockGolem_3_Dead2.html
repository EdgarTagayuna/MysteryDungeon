Dead End

As you attempted to jump back up towards the floor above, you were able to grab the ledge. However, you realized that it was a feint.
It stood up, and extended its very lengthy arms. It managed to grab hold of you, crushing your parts of your ribs. It proceeded to
throw you to the ground, breaking various parts of your body. To your surprise, you were still alive. However, from your peripheral vision,
you see the giant walking slowly towards you. You tried moving, but you couldn't. The ground shook each step, until you realized its giant feet was
next to you. In an instant, everything was dark as the giant rock golem stepped on you repeatedly, resulting in a puddle of blood.


Quite the unfortunate turn of events.
