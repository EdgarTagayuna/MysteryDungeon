Walls

Traversing the pathway, it seemed to strech endlessly towards a very dark, singular point.
As you continued on foot, all you could hear was your footsteps and the sound of your heart beating.
Just as you were walking, in an instant, both sides of the walls compressed your entirety, causing
you to be splattered into a bloody pulp.


You shouldn't have been so careless. May this be a lesson for you.
